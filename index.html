Roasting

Coffee begins its journey with the roasting of green beans. Expert roasters monitor temperature and time, transforming raw beans into aromatic, flavorful coffee.

Grinding

The next step is grinding the roasted beans. Precision is key: coarse grinds suit French press brewing, while fine grinds are ideal for espresso.

Brewing

With freshly ground coffee ready, the brewing process begins. Water temperature, brewing method, and extraction time all play crucial roles in crafting the perfect cup.
